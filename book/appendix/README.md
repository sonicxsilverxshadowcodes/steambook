# Appendix – Going Further

This appendix has extra topics to spark curiosity and take you one step beyond the main book.

- **A1: Semiconductors** – What they are, why they matter, and where we find them.
- **A2: Arduino** – A small board that can control lights, motors, and sensors.
- **A3: Raspberry Pi** – A tiny computer for projects and learning.
- **A4: Embedded Electronics** – Electronics inside everyday devices.
- **A5: How a Mobile App Is Made** – From idea to the app on your phone.
- **A6: How a Game Is Made** – How games are designed and built.
- **A7: How a Website Is Made** – How web pages are created and shown in a browser.

Each section is kept short and at a “basics” level so you can explore more on your own.
